QuackGIS is being deployed!

1. Wait for the pod to become ready:
   kubectl wait pod -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "quackgis.name" . }} --for=condition=ready --timeout=120s

2. Get the connection string:
   export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "quackgis.name" . }} -o jsonpath='{.items[0].metadata.name}')
   kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 55432:5432

3. Connect with psql:
   psql postgres://{{ .Values.quackgis.postgresUser }}:{{ .Values.quackgis.postgresPassword }}@127.0.0.1:55432/{{ .Values.quackgis.postgresDb }}

4. Run a spatial query:
   SELECT st_astext(st_geomfromtext('POINT(0 0)'));

DuckLake storage: {{ .Values.quackgis.ducklake.mode }} mode at /var/lib/quackgis
